One Piece Project

I created a web page dedicated to the characters of One Piece,
in collaboration with the Dev em Dobro channel on YouTube,
as part of an introductory exercise in HTML5 and CSS3.

an attractive manner. This experience allowed me to apply and
reinforce my basic knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3, learning

Scratch mini game

During the CS50's Understanding Technology course at Harvard,
taught by Professor David J. Malan, I developed a mini-game in

concepts and explore creativity while celebrating Michael Jackson's

Boa Ação

I am currently working on a team project to develop a website
for donations and item exchanges, aligned with the
United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs),
specifically SDGs 1, 11, and 12. Our goal is to facilitate
donations and exchange of items to help underprivileged
communities, promoting the sustainable redistribution of resources.
We are focusing on features such as profile creation, item listing
and search, and a secure system for donations and exchanges.
This project aims to foster solidarity and sustainability while
applying our technical knowledge to contribute to a fairer world.
